MISSION & VISION

PURPOSE

This project aims to raise awareness about digital addiction and encourage students to develop healthier and more responsible digital habits. Through awareness campaigns, digital detox activities, peer support, and practical guidelines. And by these initiatives it can help students reduce excessive screen time, improve self-discipline, and use technology more productively.

MISSION

To raise awareness among students about the effects of excessive digital usage and promote responsible technology use through educational campaigns, digital tools, and collaborative activities that support academic success and student well-being.

VISION

A learning environment where students are aware of the impact of digital addiction and maintain a healthy balance between digital engagement and academic responsibilities. Students become disciplined, productive, and responsible digital citizens who use technology to enhance learning and personal growth.

WHAT THE PLATFORM OFFERS

The Digital Awareness Addiction Hub is a school-based initiative designed to promote responsible technology use and reduce digital addiction among students. This project features an integrated digital ecosystem composed of a central educational website and a strategic social media campaign that encourages students to shift from passive consumption to active and disciplined technology use.

Learning Resources

Interactive self-assessment tools, productivity software, and ICT learning modules help students track screen habits and improve academic collaboration.

Awareness Campaign

Social media platforms deliver bite-sized content about digital addiction and promote engagement challenges that encourage healthier tech habits.

Habit Development

Students are encouraged to set screen limits, take breaks, and prioritize schoolwork to develop healthier routines and improve well-being.

Integrated Experience

Social media directs students to the website for deeper learning, creating a unified platform that promotes productive technology use.
